3. App Purpose and Features

FilmStock is designed to help you:

  • Manage your analog film stock inventory, including individual rolls with expiry dates, frozen status, exposure counts, and notes

  • Organize your collection with filtering and sorting
  • Take or select photos of your film boxes for reference
