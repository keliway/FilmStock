Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026
App: FilmStock

Introduction

FilmStock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

FilmStock does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you create in the app is stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to your data at any time.

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • Film stock inventory and details (manufacturer, type, format, speed, expiry dates, notes, roll counts, frozen status, exposure counts)
  • Custom photos of film boxes taken with the camera or selected from your photo library
  • App settings and preferences (language, filters, sorting, enabled formats)
  • Films currently loaded in your cameras (loading dates, custom ISO settings, roll details)
  • Camera information (names, assigned film formats, associated films)
  • Finished film records (completion dates, development status, roll details)
  • Widget configuration data

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Export and Import

FilmStock provides an optional Export & Import feature (currently in beta) that allows you to export your film inventory as a JSON or CSV file. These files are created locally on your device and shared only through the standard iOS share sheet — you control exactly where they go. We do not receive copies of any exported files. Imported files are read locally and never transmitted.

Camera and Photo Library Access

FilmStock requests the following permissions:

  • Camera Access: To take photos of your film boxes for visual reference. These photos are processed locally, cropped to square format if needed, and stored only on your device.
  • Photo Library Access: To select existing photos from your device to use as film images. Selected photos are copied into the app's local storage and never uploaded or shared.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through iOS Settings. The app will continue to function without these permissions, but you won't be able to add custom images.

Data Processing

All data processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Image cropping and resizing for film photos
  • Filtering and sorting of your film collection
  • Widget data updates for the home screen
  • Statistics calculations
  • Export file generation (JSON/CSV)
  • Import file parsing and validation

No data is sent to external servers for processing.

In-App Purchases

FilmStock offers an optional in-app purchase ("Buy Me a Coffee") to support development. This transaction is processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

FilmStock uses the following Apple services:

  • StoreKit for in-app purchases
  • WidgetKit for home screen widgets
  • SwiftData for local data persistence
  • AVFoundation for camera functionality
  • PhotosUI for photo library access

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with anyone. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no data for us to share.

Children's Privacy

FilmStock does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children specifically.

Data Retention and Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual films, cameras, or records at any time through the app
  • Delete all app data by deleting the FilmStock app from your device
  • Your data is included in iOS backups (if enabled) and can be restored when restoring your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be noted in the app's What's New section.
